THE BEGINNER

Walking into a gym can be overwhelming. Trying to figure out what do, how to use the equipment, and even which equipment to use can be quite a daunting experience. We’re sensitive to that and we want to make getting started as painless and enjoyable as possible, dare we say fun! Working with a certified personal trainer removes all of the guess work. Our studio offers privacy so there's no reason to feel self conscious or reason to worry that someone is watching and judging you. Without the crowds of a traditional gym you are better able to focus on your workout and your trainer is better able to focus on you. Your Personal Trainer will create an individualized program that will most effectively and most efficiently help you to achieve your fitness goals. Your personal trainer will work closely with you to continuously guide and modify your training program. This ensures that you are getting the most out of your workout.

SENIORS

As we get older our needs and abilities change and we can't do the things we once did with the same ease. This doesn't mean you have to take it lying down! Exercise can help you to stay strong and fit so you can keep enjoying the things that you like to do. Physical activity is one of the most powerful anti-aging tools we have. Some have even called it the Fountain of Youth. Regular exercise enhances your chances of remaining mobile and independent for years to come. Our experienced personal trainers understand our evolving needs as we age and are here to help. Studies have shown that regular exercise can help prevent or delay many diseases and disabilities. In some cases exercise is an effective treatment for many chronic conditions. For example studies show that people with arthritis, heart disease, high blood pressure, or diabetes benefit from regular exercise. Exercise also helps people with balance problems or difficulty walking.

YOUTH

It's never too early to start developing good habits and re-enforcing healthy attitudes towards fitness. Besides enjoying the health benefits of regular exercise, kids who are physically fit sleep better and are better able to handle the physical and emotional challenges they will face - from running to catch the bus to studying for a test. Whether your child wants to be the star of the team, just wants a little more playing time or simply wants to be more active, we can help them reach their goals.

We know parents have concerns, and just like every parent, you want the best for your child. While you want your child to be heathy and enjoy exercising, you probably have concerns about their abilities and safety. This is where we can help. Safety is always our paramount concern. We give children exercises that are appropriate for their age and development. We use positive reinforcement to ensure that your child feels good about his or her accomplishments and has a positive self body image. After a short time you'll see the improvement in your child's athletic performance and a boost in their self confidence.
