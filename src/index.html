What makes us DIFFERENT? We're more than a workout

Just steps from the Irvington train station, PUSH is your escape from the crowded, chaotic gym scene.

We opened in 2012 with a simple idea: create a space where people actually feel comfortable working out. No judgment, no pressure, no trying to figure out if you're doing something right while someone watches from across the room (spoiler: they're not).

At PUSH, it's just you and your trainer — working together in a clean, modern space that's all about real focus and real results. No noise, no egos, no distractions.

Our certified trainers (NASM) work with people of all ages and fitness levels. Whether you're brand new, coming back after an injury, or training for something specific, we'll build a plan around you — your body, your goals, your schedule.

This is personal training the way it should be: relaxed, supportive, and built to actually work.

What You'll Find at PUSH:

  • A supportive, non-intimidating environment
  • Customized programs that deliver results
  • State-of-the-art equipment in a bright, spacious studio
  • Expert guidance from top-tier certified trainers

PUSH isn't just a place to work out — it's where you show up, put in the work, and feel good doing it.
